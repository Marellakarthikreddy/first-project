Dhoni was seen sporting a white t-shirt and a black mask as he made his way out of the Chennai airport in the late evening. MS Dhoni has made it a habit to arrive in Chennai well ahead of an Indian Premier League season to be part of training camps and prepare for auctions. The former India captain traveled to Chennai before the team jetted off to the UAE for IPL editions over the last three years. Dhoni has been busy shooting commercials in the lead-up to the T20 World Cup. The World Cup-winning former skipper created a buzz when he took to his Facebook post and teased fans about a special announcement in September. In the end, it turned out to be an advertisement for a popular biscuit brand with a World Cup twist.
